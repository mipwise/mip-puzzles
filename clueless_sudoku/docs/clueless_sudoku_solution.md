# Clueless Sudoku Puzzle - Solution

The sum of the digits in every region is 9.

The unique solution is the following:

|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|
| 4   | 3   | 1   | 2   | 5   | 6   |
| 1   | 5   | 4   | 6   | 2   | 3   |
| 6   | 2   | 5   | 3   | 1   | 4   |
| 3   | 6   | 2   | 5   | 4   | 1   |
| 2   | 1   | 6   | 4   | 3   | 5   |
| 5   | 4   | 3   | 1   | 6   | 2   |
